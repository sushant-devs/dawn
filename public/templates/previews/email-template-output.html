Important Safety Information

CONTRAINDICATIONS: None.

WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS: Thrombotic microangiopathy (TMA) and thromboembolism have been observed when HEMLIBRA was used concomitantly with activated prothrombin complex concentrate (aPCC). Avoid use of aPCC while on HEMLIBRA.

ADVERSE REACTIONS: The most common adverse reactions (≥10%) were injection site reactions, headache, and arthralgia.

DRUG INTERACTIONS: Do not administer aPCC with HEMLIBRA unless clinical need outweighs risk.
